Waffles

Easy breakfast waffles topped with strawberries, blueberries and raspberries, drizzled with maple syrup.

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 6 mins

Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 150g self raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 250ml milk
  • 1 egg
  • 20g butter

Method

  1. Put the flour, baking powder and caster sugar into a mixing bowl and stir gently to combine.
  2. Make a well in the middle of the dry ingredients and crack in the egg - add a little of the milk and using a wooden spoon - stirring in small circles - gradually mix together the batter - adding more milk whenever it becomes stiff. Eventually you'll have a smooth rich batter - pour this into a jug it just makes it so much easier for pouring into the waffle maker.
  3. Heat up the waffle maker - I gently brush the waffle plates with a little melted butter and then pour in the batter. Cook these for around 5-6 minutes and don't even look at them before 4 minutes is up.
  4. Once cooked, flip them from the waffle maker using a plastic spatula so you don't scratch the waffle plates.
  5. Once cooked you can serve them with strawberries, raspberries and blueberries and drizzle with maple syrup.

Nutritional Information: per serving

  • Calories: 224 kcal
  • Fat: 7g
  • Saturates: 4g
  • Sodium: 653mg
  • Carbohydrates: 33g
  • Sugars: 1g
  • Fibre: 1g
  • Protein: 8g
