Super Berry Smoothie

Frozen berries are a thrifty way of creating a healthy smoothie - pad it out with oats to make it extra filling.

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 0 mins

Serves 4

Ingredients

Add to a high powered blender:

  • 450g frozen berries
  • 450g fat-free strawberry yoghurt
  • 100ml milk
  • 25g porridge oat (optional)
  • 2 tsp honey (optional)

Method

  1. Whizz the berries, yoghurt and milk together with a stick blender until smooth. To make it extra filling add the porridge oats to the blender.
  2. Pour the smoothie into 4 glasses and serve with a drizzle of honey, if you like.

Nutritional Information: per serving

  • Calories: 117 kcal
  • Fat: 1g
  • Saturates: 1g
  • Carbs: 18g
  • Sugars: 14g
  • Fibre: 4g
  • Protein: 8g
  • Salt: 0.2g
