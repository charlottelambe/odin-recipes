Salami and Chorizo Pizza

A simple but delicious pizza, with salami sausage, chorizo picante & mozzarella cheese.

Prep Time: 20 mins plus proving

Cook Time: 10 mins

Makes 4 medium pizzas

Ingredients

    For the dough:

  • 500 grams strong white bread flour, preferable 00
  • 1/2 tbsp salt
  • 7 grams dried yeast
  • 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 325 ml lukewarm water

    For the toppings:

  • 20 thin slices of salami sausage
  • 32 thin slices chorizo picante
  • 2 balls mozzarella cheese
  • olive oil
  • fresh basil leaves to garnish

Method

  1. To make the pizza dough, combine yeast, olive oil, water and sugar, whisk with a fork to resolve and set aside for a few minutes.
  2. Sift the flour with the salt, make a well in the middle and pour the yeast-mixture in the well. Stir the flour with a fork from the inner edge of the well with a circular movement into the water-yeast mixture.
  3. Continue to mix, bringing in all the flour until the dough comes together and becomes too hard to mix with a fork.
  4. Flour your hands and knead the dough on a floured surface for at least 10 minutes until a smooth and soft dough.
  5. Place the dough into a lightly greased bowl, cover with a moist kitchen towel and let it rise in a warm place for about an hour, until doubled in volume.
  6. Preheat the oven to 250C.
  7. Put the dough on a floured surface and knead for 30 seconds, then divide into 4 balls. Roll each ball out into a circle, about 0.5 cm thick.
  8. To make the pizza, roll out 1 pizza dough ball
  9. Arrange salami slices and chorizo slices over the pizza dough, and tear over the mozzarella cheese. Drizzle with a little olive oil and bake in a preheated oven for about 10 minutes until golden and crispy.
  10. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve.

Tips

  • This pizza dough recipe can be used with alternative toppings of your choice such as chicken, prosciutto, mushrooms and peppers.
