Seafood Pasta

This is not your ordinary weeknight pasta. It's a special occasion pasta. Seafood Pasta is the perfect way to impress your friends and family and it's actually really easy!

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 25 mins

Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 350 g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp. butter
  • 225 g prawns, peeled and deveined
  • 225 g scallops, patted dry
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 small shallot, crushed
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 180 ml milk
  • 180 ml low-sodium chicken or vegetable stock
  • 50 g freshly grated Parmesan, plus more for serving
  • 200 g chopped tomatoes
  • 5 g freshly chopped parsley, plus more for garnish
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon

Method

  1. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook spaghetti according to package directions until al dente. Drain and return to the pot.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large pan over medium heat, melt butter. Add prawns and scallops and season with salt and pepper. Cook 2 minutes per side, then transfer to a plate.
  3. To pan add shallot and garlic, cook until soft and fragrant, 3 minutes. Add milk, stock, Parmesan, tomatoes, and parsley and season with salt. Simmer 3 minutes more, then return the cooked prawns and scallops and toss until combined. Squeeze with lemon juice.
  4. Add cooked spaghetti and toss until fully coated.
  5. Garnish with parsley and serve with parmesan.
